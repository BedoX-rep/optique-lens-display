# Brand Design System: Modern Minimalist

## Core Philosophy
- **Clean & Contemporary**: Focus on whitespace, strong typography, and high-quality imagery.
- **Minimalist**: Remove unnecessary decorations, gradients, and bright colors (especially teal).
- **Premium**: Use a monochrome palette with subtle textures or very muted accents.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Foreground (Text)**: Dark Gray (`#111827` - gray-900)
- **Primary Action**: Black (`#000000`) or Very Dark Gray (`#18181b` - zinc-950)

### Secondary Colors
- **Secondary Background**: Light Gray (`#F9FAFB` - gray-50)
- **Border**: Light Gray (`#E5E7EB` - gray-200)
- **Muted Text**: Medium Gray (`#6B7280` - gray-500)

### Accent (Minimal Use)
- Used only for focus states or critical alerts.
- **Focus/Active**: A slightly lighter gray or a very subtle metallic tone.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Clean Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, system-ui). Light to Medium weights.
  - H1: `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-light tracking-tight`
  - H2: `text-3xl font-light`
  - H3: `text-2xl font-normal`
- **Body**: Clean Sans-Serif.
  - Base: `text-base text-gray-600 leading-relaxed`
  - Small: `text-sm text-gray-500`

## UI Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Black background, White text. `rounded-lg`. Hover: Dark Gray.
- **Secondary**: White background, Black border, Black text. `rounded-lg`. Hover: Light Gray bg.
- **Ghost**: Transparent background, Black text. Hover: Light Gray bg.

### Cards
- Minimal borders (`border border-gray-100`).
- Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm` or `hover:shadow-md`).
- Clean white backgrounds.

### Navigation
- White background.
- Minimal border bottom.
- Black text links.
- Simple icons.

## Implementation Guide (Tailwind)
- Remove `teal-*` and `cyan-*` classes.
- Replace with `gray-*`, `zinc-*`, or `black`/`white`.
- Use `bg-white` for sections.
- Use `text-gray-900` for headings.
- Use `text-gray-500` for body text.
